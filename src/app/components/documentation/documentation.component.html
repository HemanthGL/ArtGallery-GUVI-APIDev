Create an Account at our Portal to get access to like and save your favourite artworks to get back to them whenever you want, wherever you want. Your Favourite piece would be shared specific, locally to your account so that you never miss them at any moment, wherever you are, whichever devices you're on.


We've utilized reactive Forms in Angular to help guide you through the basic requirements of your account credentials, i.e., unique username of min 4 char length, and max 15 char length, and matching set of passwords with min. 8 character length.


Form Details:
  • Username: unique to your account
  • Password: create a strong password of min. 8 character length
  • Confirm Password: Before you get on, confirm your password one last time..
